Development Resources

 

Here are a couple of telnet source resources out on the web. If you know of others, please send them to webmaster@telnet.org.

If you are looking for something to help you with an assignment, please do not plagiarize this source.  You'll be much better off learning to do things yourself.  If you are a professor of such a class, feel free to contact us at webmaster@telnet.org

Telnet source code:

Console Telnet for win32 - source forge project:
http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=4675&release_id=13905

Surf around here for source for NCSA Telnet on Mac and PC:
http://www.ncsa.uiuc.edu/SDG/Homepage/telnet.html
